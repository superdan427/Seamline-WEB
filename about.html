About

Scrolling. Comparing. Ego-maxing. Saving posts. Saving sewing guides. Saving “inspiration” you’ll never open again. It feels like doing something. It isn’t.

Seamline was created to get you outside. Touch some grass. Feel some actual fabric. Find small shops you didn’t know existed.

It’s a directory that gets you off your phone and into the real world. Fabric shops, suppliers, tailors. Places and people fashion is built on and depends on.

Seamline is a starting point. Pick a place. Go there. Touch the fabric. Make something.

We’re not here for aesthetic posturing. Seamline helps you find fabric shops so you can start making clothes. Simple as.

Because as we all know fashion students are the most important people in the world.

They know everything about everything.

And without them, especially the East London creatives, civilisation would collapse.

Less content. Less ego. Less gatekeeping.

Make something.
